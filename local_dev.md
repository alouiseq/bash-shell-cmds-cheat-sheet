# Local Dev CheatSheet

## NerdTree

`:NERDTree` **: Open NerdTree (VIM)** 
`o` **: open in window**
`i` **: open in split window**
`s` **: open in vsplit window**
`t` **: open in new tab**
`ctrl-w(2x)` **: switch to the next window**
`ctrl-w h` **: switch to the previous window**
`gt` **: switch to the next tab**
